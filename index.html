Final Year Internships

Real industry experience in two high-demand domains — for ECE & EEE final year students.

ECEEEE
⚡

Power Electronics Internship

Design, simulate and prototype real power converter circuits under industry mentors — from DC-DC converters to motor drives.

What you'll learn
  • DC-DC Converters — Buck, Boost & Buck-Boost design & simulation
  • Inverter topologies — Single-phase & three-phase PWM inverters
  • MPPT algorithms for Solar PV (Perturb & Observe, Incremental Conductance)
  • Motor drives — Variable Frequency Drive (VFD) control techniques
  • MATLAB/Simulink modelling and hardware prototyping
  • EV Battery Management System fundamentals
1–3 Months Certificate Stipend Eligible Hardware Access IEEE Paper Support
Apply — Power Electronics
ECEEEE
🔬

VLSI Design Internship

Go from RTL to GDSII. Gain hands-on experience in digital IC design, FPGA implementation and industry-standard EDA tools.

What you'll learn
  • Digital design fundamentals — combinational & sequential circuits in Verilog
  • RTL design and functional simulation using ModelSim / Vivado
  • FPGA implementation on Xilinx / Intel boards
  • ASIC design flow — synthesis, placement & routing basics
  • Low-power design techniques and timing analysis (STA)
  • Mini project: AES / RISC-V processor / Image processing core
1–3 Months Certificate Stipend Eligible EDA Tool Access FPGA Board Access
